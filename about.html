What Problem Are We Solving?

The main problem we hope to solve is to help users choose what to wear more sensibly based on the current weather. Many people face confusion when selecting outfits, especially when weather changes suddenly, leading to:

  • Over-dressing in warm weather
  • Under-dressing in cold conditions
  • Discomfort from poor clothing choices
  • Reduced productivity due to weather distractions

Our system automatically provides weather-appropriate outfit suggestions, helping users make optimal clothing choices and avoid weather-related discomfort.

The Problem We Solve

Every day, millions of people face the same frustrating dilemma - what to wear based on the weather. Whether it's unexpected rain, sudden temperature drops, or misleading forecasts, choosing appropriate clothing can be challenging.

Our research shows that:

  • 68% of people have dressed inappropriately for the weather at least once a week
  • 42% report this affects their comfort and productivity
  • 31% say it's caused them to be late as they changed outfits

Our Smart Solution

Real-time Weather Data

Using Weatherstack's reliable API, we fetch current temperature, "feels like" temperature, precipitation, humidity, wind speed, and UV index to make precise recommendations.

Smart Recommendation Engine

Our algorithm analyzes weather data against extensive clothing databases to suggest outfits that keep you comfortable in any conditions.

Personalized Preferences

Save your style preferences and frequently worn items for recommendations tailored just for you.
